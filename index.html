YingHong Yuan

Artist Statement

YingHong Yuan is a Digital Media Art student at San Jose State University, CA. Her hometown is Guangzhou, China. YingHong's artworks inspired by her reflection on some social problems and the expression of personal feelings. Most of her works are digital images made by Illustrator or Photoshop, HTML and P5.JS based on Web Design, and art video. She also expanded more areas of digital media art, such as installation art. She considers the social problem and put her thought into the artworks represents to the audience by the visual effect.
